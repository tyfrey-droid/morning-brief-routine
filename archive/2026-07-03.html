🧬 Mito Research
  • A June 24 imaging study in Neuroimage: Reports from Peking University First Hospital analyzed 61 MRI scans from 55 MELAS patients (2013-2024) to model how acute stroke-like lesions spread across the cortex. Lesions spread freely within a brain region but are sharply bounded at its edges, and this boundary pattern tracks with local synaptic density — sparser areas act as barriers. It's a purely mechanistic finding, not a treatment, but it's new insight specific to the stroke-like episodes that are Emi's most acute symptom, and could eventually help clinicians better interpret and predict how these episodes evolve on imaging. (Neuroimage: Reports)
