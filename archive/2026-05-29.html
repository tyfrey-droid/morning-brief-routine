🧬 Mito Research
  • A comprehensive review published May 28 in Molecular Biomedicine (Springer Nature, Chinese University of Hong Kong) surveys the rapidly evolving treatment landscape for mitochondrial dysfunction across neurodegenerative conditions. It examines gene-targeted therapies — including viral and non-viral vectors designed to correct mtDNA mutations — along with organelle replacement approaches and emerging epitranscriptomic interventions that modify RNA rather than DNA directly, potentially sidestepping the longstanding challenges of editing the mitochondrial genome.
