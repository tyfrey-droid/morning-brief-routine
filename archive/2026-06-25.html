  • A letter published June 18 in Revista Española de Enfermedades Digestivas by neurologist Josef Finsterer examines the clinical challenges when the m.3243A>G MELAS mutation also causes chronic intestinal pseudo-obstruction (CIPO) — a serious GI complication that can accompany the neurological symptoms.
